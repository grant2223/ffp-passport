About FFP

Built in the UAE.
For the people who move.

FFP started as a way for active people to find each other. Today it’s a community-first membership for everyone in the UAE who trains, competes, travels and wants real connection — not just another deals app.

The story.

FFP — Find Fit People — was born from a simple frustration. Active people moving to the UAE found training partners hard to come by. Gyms were full, classes were busy, but the connections weren’t happening. People worked out alone next to each other.

So we built the thing that didn’t exist: a passport that connects you to others who train, compete and explore the way you do. One annual membership. One growing community. One way to stop training alone.

What started with a small group of runners and padel players has grown into the UAE’s most active community — people who turn up, train hard, and bring others with them.

What FFP stands for.

Four pillars that shape every event, partnership and feature we build.

01

People First

Real connection beats real discounts. We exist to help people meet and move together — deals are a perk, not the point.

02

Active Lifestyle

Strength, endurance, recovery, wellness. Whatever your sport, FFP is built for the people who train for life, not for a season.

03

UAE Rooted

Built here, by people who train here. From Dubai gyms to Hatta trails to Abu Dhabi courts — FFP knows the local scene.

04

Show Up

Membership rewards the people who do the work. Train, lead, refer, host — FFP celebrates and pays the people who turn up.

Founder

Grant Hermanus

Founder · FFP Passport

New Zealand born, Dubai based. Grant spent years building active communities — trail runs, sports events, fitness groups — before turning that work into FFP.

“FFP exists because I kept hearing the same thing: I want to train with people, I just don’t know where to find them. The passport is the answer to that. One place where active people connect, organise and show up.”
