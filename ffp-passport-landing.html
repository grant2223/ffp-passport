Five ways in

Match with people. Host or join meetups. Tap into professional networks. Get into provider events. Travel together for full experiences. Each one a different door to people, places and possibilities.

01 — FOUNDATION
Matched to people like you
We identify members near you with similar sports, similar level and similar interests. You decide who to connect with — it's always your call.
A 4am running partner in JLT. A weekend padel duo at your level. A yoga buddy in your neighbourhood.

Meet & Move
Create or join a small-group meetup, up to 8 people. The simplest way to get a session on the calendar with people in your community. Some are free, some have a small cost — either way, you're in.
A 4th for padel. A kayak crew. A morning run. A kite-surf session. A beach volleyball game.

Coaches sharing methodology. Pros training with pros. Entrepreneurs in the active-life space, meeting their tribe.

Events
Providers opening their doors to people who love the lifestyle. Try out their services, meet their team, find your next studio, club or coach.
A taster class at a new studio. A community race weekend. A coaching clinic with a touring pro.
