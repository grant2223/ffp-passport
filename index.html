FIND YOUR PEOPLE
What the passport opens

Five ways in.

Match with people. Host or join meet-ups. Tap into professional networks. Get into provider events. Travel together for full experiences. Each one a different door to people, places and possibilities.

01 — Foundation
Matched to people like you
We identify members near you with similar sports, similar level and similar interests. You decide who to connect with — always your call.
A 4am running partner in JLT. A weekend padel duo at your level. A yoga buddy in your neighbourhood.
02 — Member-hosted
Meet & Move
Create or join a small-group meet-up, up to 8 people. The simplest way to get a session on the calendar with people in your community.
A 4th for padel. A kayak crew. A morning run. A kite-surf session. A beach volleyball game.
03 — Professionals
Pro Network
A dedicated space for coaches, professional athletes, entrepreneurs and serious practitioners who want to connect with their peers.
Coaches sharing methodology. Pros training with pros. Entrepreneurs in the active-life space.
04 — Provider-led
Events
Providers opening their doors to people who love the lifestyle. Try their services, meet their team, find your next studio, club or coach.
A taster class at a new studio. A community race weekend. A coaching clinic with a touring pro.
05 — Multi-day & travel
Experiences
Retreats, training camps, and curated travel to major sporting events worldwide — F1, golf majors, marathons, padel tournaments, Rugby Sevens.
Outbound trips with your crew. Inbound itineraries for visitors coming to UAE events.
