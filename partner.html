How It Works

Two ways to partner with FFP.

Start free with a listing that automatically becomes a Member Perk on FFP Passport. Upgrade when you’re ready to host events, experiences and challenges for our community.

01
Start Here · Always Free

List Your Business.

Create a free listing on findfitpeople.com — the UAE’s sports, fitness and wellness directory. Your listing becomes a Member Perk on FFP Passport automatically.

  • Unlimited service listings
  • Member Perk badge on FFP Passport (bring-a-friend, member rate, etc.)
  • Self-managed provider account
  • Free promo video (limited-time introductory offer)
02
Upgrade Anytime

Host Events & Experiences.

Pro Members and Featured Partners unlock the ability to host Events, Experiences and Challenges for FFP Passport members — the most engaged active-lifestyle audience in the UAE.

  • Run community events at your venue
  • Offer experiences & group trips
  • Create challenges with leaderboards
  • Sales boosters & promo support from the FFP team

From the Founder

Grant Goes

Founder · FFP

I’ve spent 15 years living and breathing the active lifestyle in the UAE. Training, competing, coaching, presenting, and connecting with some of the most passionate sports, fitness and wellness people in this city.

What I’ve seen — every single day — is how much this community gives to the people in it. Better health. Better performance. Better quality of life.

FFP exists to connect those people with the businesses that support them. To make it easier for anyone who wants to live actively to find exactly the right support — and to make sure the brilliant providers delivering that support get the recognition, the reach, and the customers they deserve.

“Active people live better. Let’s help more people live that way.”
