How It Works

From signup to live in three steps.

No applications, no waiting to get started. Create your account, build your listings, and our team reviews them before they go live to members.

1

Create your free account

Sign up in under a minute — business name, your details and location. Confirm your email and you’re straight into your provider dashboard.

2

Build your listings

Add your Events, Experiences and Challenges with photos, details and pricing. Everything stays private to you while you build it out.

3

We approve & members discover you

Our team reviews each listing for quality, then publishes it to the FFP Passport community — the active people most likely to show up.

What You Can Run

Three ways to bring the community to you.

List as much or as little as you like — all from one self-managed dashboard.

Events

Run community sessions, classes and meet-ups at your venue. Members RSVP through the app and turn up ready to move.

Experiences

Offer retreats, group trips and signature sessions. Set your dates, capacity and pricing — members apply and book.

Challenges

Create challenges that keep members coming back — goals, metrics and prizes that drive repeat visits to your business.
